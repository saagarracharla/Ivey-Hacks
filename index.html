Ivey x Cyder Hackathon

What is this

One of a kind hybrid business hackathon to give business students the chance to work with technical students in a shared space to ideate, design and develop product solutions!

Half Ivey + Half Computer Science

This balance students to effectively work together and create teams that are balanced in both disciplines

48 hours

Attendees will get 48 hours to create a solution , kicking things off with zoom and slowly making out way into the Ivey building Friday evening.
